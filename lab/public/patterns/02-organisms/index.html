Who We Are

UX Akron is a user experience (UX) meetup community in Northeast Ohio, primarily serving members from Akron, Canton, Youngstown and Kent. Anyone is welcome at UX Akron - from students and UX professionals to freelancers and those just interested in UX. Join our next meeting to get a taste of the great events we offer all year.
