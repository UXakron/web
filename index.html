Who We Are

UX Akron is a user experience meetup community in Northeast Ohio, primarily serving members from Akron, Canton, Youngstown and Kent. Anyone is welcome at UX Akron from students and UX professionals to freelancers those who are interested in UX. Join our next meeting and see what great events we have to offer.
